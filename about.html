About Aurevo

Where Arabica, music, and people come together in the heart of your neighborhood.

Our Story

Aurevo started with a simple idea: create a coffee shop that feels like home. Founded in 2019 by two friends who shared a love for specialty coffee and vinyl records, we opened our doors on Maple Street with nothing but a secondhand espresso machine and a dream.

Today, Aurevo is a gathering place for the neighborhood — remote workers, students, and longtime regulars who've made our corner booth their own. Every cup we serve is a small act of hospitality.

What We Believe

Three pillars that guide everything we do.

Quality First

We source directly from farmers who share our commitment to sustainable, ethical growing practices.

Made with Love

Every latte is poured by hand, every pastry baked fresh each morning in our kitchen.

Open to All

Everyone is welcome — including your four-legged friends. Pull up a chair and stay as long as you like.
